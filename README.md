 Smart Agriculture Data Analytics Project 

Can data science help solve food security challenges?I explored this by analyzing 20+ years of India’s agricultural data (2001–2023), combining crop production with market price trends to uncover insights into yield efficiency, seasonal contributions, and growth cycles.

🔹 Key Achievements:

Built national agriculture KPIs: 1.17B tonnes production, 2.68 tonnes/ha average yield, 20 states, 25 crops.

Conducted decade-wise growth analysis → revealed a sharp decline (-60.5%) in the 2020s.

Visualized seasonal production shares: Kharif (40.1%), Rabi (40.0%), Whole Year (20%).

Identified top 10 crops by production (Linseed, Coconut, Soyabean, Rice, etc.) using treemaps.

Analyzed yield efficiency trends for top 5 states, uncovering stagnation and slight declines in CAGR.

Combined production + market price datasets to highlight economic sustainability challenges.

🔹 Key Learnings:

Advanced skills in Python, Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib, Squarify.

Strengthened data cleaning, transformation, and feature engineering (yield per hectare).

Learned to translate analytics into business-ready insights with impactful visual storytelling.

Gained perspective on agriculture economics & sustainability, transferable to finance, retail, and supply chain analytics.

💡 Takeaway:This project sharpened my ability to turn raw data into actionable narratives—a skill I’m excited to apply in real-world data science & business analytics roles.
